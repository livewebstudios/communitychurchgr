If you or a family member are hospitalized, please call the office so they can arrange a pastoral visit. Please note, hospitals do not take the initiative to schedule these visits.

We invite you to submit prayer requests to the church. You can call the office at (201) 444-4625 or email Pastor Mark at pastor@communitychurchgr.com.
