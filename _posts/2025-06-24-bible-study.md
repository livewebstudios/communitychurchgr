Bible Study on Wednesday Nights

Please feel free to join any Wednesday Evening via Zoom at 8 pm.

The group will be open for both men and women and all ages are welcome.

The purpose of the study is to build God's church together here at the Glen Rock Community Church and beyond.

The study will be led by Rev. Dr. Pamela Pater-Ennis
