Please consider donating your pre-loved items to the Community Thrift Shop in Midland Park and mention or mark your bags CHARITY #3. A portion of the proceeds will go to CCGR.

Location: 85 Godwin Ave., Midland Park (201) 785-4189

Items accepted are: in-season clothes, shoes & accessories, small household items, children's toys, books (hard cover must have jacket), paperbacks in good condition, etc.

Note: Limit of donation items per dropoff — 2 boxes, or 3 bags (kitchen size).

Donations accepted – Monday 9 a.m. – 12 noon

Shopping Hours: (Cash Only) – Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday – 10 a.m. – 4 p.m.

Please call the church office with any questions (201) 444-4625
