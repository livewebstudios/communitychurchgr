Programs & Ministries

Our Programs

Lenten Events

Pot-Luck Lunches in the drop-in center on Sunday, 11 a.m., following worship. Exploring Fruit and Gifts of the Spirit series.

Bible Study

Wednesday Evening Bible Study via Zoom at 8 pm. Open to men and women of all ages. Led by Rev. Dr. Pamela Pater-Ennis.

Communion Sundays

The first Sunday of each month we receive the Sacrament of Holy Communion. Homebound communion available upon request.

Healing Sundays

Every 3rd Sunday of each month at our 10am service. Join us for healing prayers and connection.

Prayer Requests

Submit prayer requests to the church. Call (201) 444-4625 or email pastor@communitychurchgr.com.

Community Meals

Help support missions in New Jersey and Kenya. Our campus is home to Community Meals, delivering meals to our area's home-bound residents.

Outreach

Help support us with a financial contribution. We support missions in New Jersey and Kenya. Our campus is home to groups including Community Meals, delivering meals to our area's home-bound residents, and the Glen Rock Co-Op Nursery School.

Community Thrift Shop

Please consider donating your pre-loved items to the Community Thrift Shop in Midland Park and mention or mark your bags CHARITY #3. A portion of the proceeds will go to CCGR.

Location: 85 Godwin Ave., Midland Park (201) 785-4189

Items accepted: in-season clothes, shoes and accessories, small household items, children's toys, books (hard cover must have jacket), paperbacks in good condition.

Donations accepted: Monday 9 a.m. – 12 noon

Shopping Hours (Cash Only): Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday — 10 a.m. – 4 p.m.

Please call the church office with any questions (201) 444-4625
