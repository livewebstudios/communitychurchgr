Our Minister

I'm Rev. Mark William Ennis

God called me to ministry when I was four years old, and I have been an ordained minister in the Reformed Church in America for the past forty years. I have ministered in parish settings as I've nurtured, counselled, preached, and prayed with people of all ages, backgrounds, and experiences. I developed a love for storytelling as a volunteer chaplain at a Christian summer camp. Currently, I am an interim pastor and hospice chaplain. I am a preacher, teacher, weekly blogger and podcaster, as well as the author of two books, about people who are healing spiritually, with a third in process. I can help you on your journey toward spiritual growth or healing. Contact me if you would like my assistance and please hear my podcasts and read my blogs. I hope that they will bless you.

Our Mission

A Vibrant and Inclusive Spiritual Community

A vibrant and inclusive spiritual community where individuals from all walks of life can find acceptance, support, and opportunities for personal and collective growth. Rooted in the teachings of Jesus Christ, we strive to embody love, compassion, and justice in our interactions with one another and the world around us. Through meaningful worship, transformative ministries, and intentional fellowship, we seek to cultivate an environment where all are empowered to explore their faith, nurture their spiritual journey, and make a positive difference in the lives of others.
