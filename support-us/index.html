Your Generosity at Work

Every Gift Makes a Difference

Your generosity keeps the doors of Community Church of Glen Rock open — sustaining heartfelt worship, caring for our congregation, and reaching far beyond our walls. Gifts of every size support our missions in New Jersey and Kenya, our Community Meals ministry for home-bound neighbors, and the everyday work of sharing love, compassion, and hope.

Giving has never been easier. Choose whichever option below is most convenient for you, and know that every contribution is received with deep gratitude.

“Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give — for God loves a cheerful giver.” 2 Corinthians 9:7

PayPal

Scan the code or search for us on PayPal to give securely in seconds.

Venmo

Open Venmo, scan the code, and send your gift directly from your phone.

Zelle

Use Zelle through your bank to make a fast, fee-free contribution.

Prefer to Give in Person or by Mail?

You are always welcome to place your gift in the Sunday offering, or mail a check to Community Church of Glen Rock, 354 Rock Road, Glen Rock, NJ 07452. Questions about giving? Call us at (201) 444-4625 — we are happy to help.
